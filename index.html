The Designer

A.M.D. Deshan — Kandy

Deep Thinker.
Perfectionist.

I am A.M.D Deshan, a 23-year-old graphic designer based in the beautiful city of Kandy, Sri Lanka. I don't rush. I analyze, I craft, and I deliver only when every pixel aligns with the vision.

I specialize in managing color palettes, typography, layout composition, and understanding client requirements deeply. I analyze the target audience of every design to ensure the output connects and converts. Though my formal experience is still growing, I am an active freelancer serving clients across various platforms.

When a client entrusts me with their vision, I commit wholeheartedly. No matter how long it takes, I am bound to deliver 98% client satisfaction — because for me, money comes second. What truly matters is elevating my craft and seeing my clients' visions come to life.

Patience Over Speed

Great design takes time. Every project is a journey of discovery, analysis, and refinement. I never rush creativity.

Client-First Focus

My goal is not money — it is your satisfaction. I build relationships through trust and designs that exceed expectations.

Uncompromising Quality

I never accept work I cannot deliver perfectly. Every design is a reflection of my name and my promise.

I question, I explore, and I craft until the design speaks the language of your brand. No shortcuts. No compromises. Only excellence."
